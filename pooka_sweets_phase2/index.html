Our Products

Kaaju Katli

Kaju katli is a traditional Indian sweet made from ground cashews and sugar, known for its rich, melt-in-your-mouth texture and delicate, nutty flavor. It's a popular treat for festivals and special occasions.

Rasagulla

Rasagulla is a beloved Indian dessert, featuring spongy, syrup-soaked cheese balls that offer a delightful combination of sweetness and soft, airy texture. It's a classic sweet enjoyed across the country.

Jalebi

Jalebi is a popular Indian dessert made by deep-frying batter into pretzel-like spirals, which are then soaked in sugar syrup. These bright orange, syrupy treats offer a sweet and crispy indulgence.

Halwa

LHalwa is a diverse and globally enjoyed sweet dish made from various ingredients like semolina, carrots, or lentils, cooked with ghee and sugar, resulting in a rich and flavorful dessert with a melt-in-the-mouth consistency. It's a staple in many cuisines and cultures.

Rabdi

Rabdi is a luscious Indian dessert made by simmering milk until it thickens and sweetening it with sugar. It is known for its creamy texture and is often flavored with cardamom, saffron, and nuts.
